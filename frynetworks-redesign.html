How it works
Three steps to earning

No blockchain experience required. Install the software, connect your wallet, and start earning.

1
Install FEM
Download the Fry Edge Miner, register your device on the dashboard, and connect your Algorand wallet.
2
Contribute to the network
FEM runs partner integrations in the background — relaying bandwidth, storing data, serving search queries, and processing AI workloads.
3
Earn rewards
The FryMinerRewardPool distributes FRY tokens each epoch based on your device's verified contributions and active integrations.
